Llama Cup

Llama Cup is a knock off version of Camel Cup. Camel Cup is a game where you roll dice to move camels, with the objective of having the most money. The way to make money is by betting on winners of the leg (a leg ends after all camels have moved) or on winners of the overall race. You can also roll the dice, awarding one coin.

So how is my spinoff different? Great question! Llama cup works with as many players as you want, compared to the board game's mere 8 players. Another difference is that this edition is more approachable for beginners. The base game seems daunting at first, but Llama Cup simplifies the game, only showing you legal moves, and Llama Cup makes it simple to understand.
