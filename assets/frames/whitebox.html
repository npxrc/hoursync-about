White Box Gallery

The White Box Gallery is an art gallery that was drafted for the American Royal. American Royal is an organisation that specalises in horse showings and races, barbecue showings, and has been established for years.

The ACE Mentorship program of Kansas City has tasked its mentors with building a gallery for the American Royal. It needs to support multiple types of art, and should have a main exhibition room, Theatre, and classroom, amongst other requirements.

The site plan and landscape plan for our project is below.

For the White Box Gallery, we were given different plots of land to choose from (A, B, and C). We chose site A because it had a slope only on one side of the plot, making it easier for us to construct a building on the plot. We had to slightly adjust the topography of the land in order to place a building down.

In order to optimise the parking lot with the space we had, we used Civil 3D in conjunction with the Autodesk Vehicle Tracking to ensure the parking lot safety standards and was efficient. We did not allot enough space to the parking lot, so we unfortunately only had 54 spots, with 4 of them being handicap as per the Wyandotte County codes.

Overall the project was fun and we learned a lot about architects think, and how to use Revit.
