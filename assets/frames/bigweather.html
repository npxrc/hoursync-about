This weather app was created primarily because of big weather websites hogging up your computers resources.

If you have ever gone to AccuWeather, or God forbid The Weather Channel's website, you will know that getting the temperature is a tedious process, ignoring their cookie warnings, ignoring the notification requests, and scrolling until you finally find the weather.

My app is a fun and unique solution to that all. No ads, and relies on the government's weather data. It's simple, lightweight, and was a very fun project to make. I do plan on adding a barn somewhere in the background of the weather, with moving chickens. and animals

And yes, when it's raining outside, water droplets fall on the website :)
